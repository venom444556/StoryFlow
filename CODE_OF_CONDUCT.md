# Code of Conduct

This project follows the [Contributor Covenant v2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/).

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making participation in this project a welcoming and respectful experience for everyone, regardless of background or identity.

## Standards

We expect all participants to be respectful, constructive, and professional in all interactions.

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported to the project maintainers at **conduct@storyflow.dev**.

Maintainers will review and respond to all reports promptly and fairly.

## Attribution

This Code of Conduct is adapted from the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org), version 2.1.
